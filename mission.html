Gainesville Women Coders is a student run organization at the University of Florida under UF WICSE with a mission to develop a strong community for young Gainesville women to learn and excel in Computer Science education. Our outreach programs include a Gainesville branch of Girls Who Code, as well as our very own created Gainesville Coding Club which will create coding clubs in numerous middle and high schools in the Gainesville area. Our mission is to create a close community in which young girls can learn how to code together in a fun and interactive environment in our best efforts to close the gender gap within the tech industry. Our wish is for this club to foster lifelong connections in which girls can reach out and offer encouragement and friendship to fellow female coders.
